What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help improve site functionality, remember preferences, and optimize performance.

Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and enable basic features such as page navigation and secure access.

Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous and aggregated data, allowing us to improve performance and usability.

Third-Party Cookies: These cookies are set by external services we use and may be used to deliver relevant advertisements or analyze engagement. They do not store personal information directly.

Marketing Cookies: These cookies track visitors across websites to display ads that are relevant and engaging to individual users.
