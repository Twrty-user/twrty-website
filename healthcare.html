Remote Patient Monitoring software

RPM software enables healthcare providers to monitor patients' health data remotely and intervene early — improving outcomes while reducing readmissions.

Key features:

  • Data collection & transmission: Secure, encrypted health data ingestion.
  • Real-time monitoring: Live vitals dashboards for clinicians.
  • Alerts & notifications: Threshold-based early warnings.
  • EHR integration: Seamless write-back to existing records.
  • Patient engagement tools: Educational content and reminders.
  • Analytics & reporting: Trend analysis and outcomes reporting.
  • Security & compliance: HIPAA-aligned controls.
  • Device connectivity: Wearables and home medical devices.
  • Telehealth capabilities: Video consultations on the same stack.
  • Scalability: Cloud-native, ready to scale.
