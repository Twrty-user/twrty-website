How it works

From idea to published — in three steps

1

Pick a topic or idea

Type any topic, or choose from a live feed of trending Blog Ideas — each with a ready-made outline. Set the length and tone, and you're ready to generate.

2

AI writes the full blog

BlogBoat generates a complete, structured article — headings, sections and images — in seconds. Edit it your way, or send it straight to publish.

3

Publish in one click

Push it to Medium, Dev.to, Hashnode, WordPress and 15+ platforms at once — or export to Word, PDF, HTML or Markdown.

Features

More than just AI writing

BlogBoat is a full blogging workspace — from drafting and editing to translating and exporting.

Block-based AI editing

Every blog is built from blocks. Rewrite, expand or restyle any single block with AI — without touching the rest of your article.

Write manually too

Prefer to write yourself? Use the full manual editor with formatting, images and structure — AI assist is there only when you want it.

Write & publish in 16 languages

Generate, edit and publish your blog in 16 languages — reach a global audience from one place, without leaving the app.

Export anywhere

Export any blog to Word, PDF, HTML or Markdown — ready for clients, documentation or your own CMS.

Mobile app

Your blog studio, in your pocket

Write, edit and publish on the go — the full BlogBoat workflow, on iOS and Android.

Privacy-first

Your data never leaves your device

BlogBoat connects to your platforms through your device's own secure keychain. Your passwords and tokens are never uploaded — and twRty has zero access to them.

Secure keychain

Credentials are stored only on your device, inside its encrypted keychain — never on a server.

Zero access

twRty never sees your platform passwords, API keys or tokens. They simply aren't ours to read.

Nothing uploaded

Your connections stay on your device. Switch devices and you simply reconnect — your data never travels.
